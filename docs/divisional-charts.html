Feeding this into KundliGPT

Don't ask the LLM to infer astrology from a single chart. Compute the relevant Vargas first, extract structured facts, then hand all of it to the model.

"The LLM should not calculate astrology — it should explain calculations produced by your astrology engine."
Where to start

The build order for a serious clone

Five charts cover most of what people actually ask. Everything else can wait for version two.

Always include alongside them

  • Vimshottari Dasha — timing of events
  • Gochar — current planetary transits
  • Nakshatras
  • Yogas
  • Shadbala — planetary strength

Add later to broaden the system

  • D4 — property & assets
  • D7 — children & family expansion
  • D24 — education & higher studies
  • D60 — deep karmic detail (needs precise birth time)
